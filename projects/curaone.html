PROJECT OVERVIEW

One Platform.
Complete Healthcare.

CuraOne was created to modernize healthcare by bringing patients, doctors, pharmacies and hospital administrators together in one secure cloud-based platform.

MY ROLE

Full Stack Developer

Designed the complete UI, developed frontend, backend APIs, authentication and MongoDB integration.

PLATFORM

CORE FEATURES

Everything Needed
For Modern Healthcare.

CuraOne combines patient management, appointments, medical records and pharmacy services into one secure and intelligent healthcare platform.

Appointment Booking

Schedule appointments, manage doctor availability and streamline patient visits.

Doctor Dashboard

Manage consultations, patient history, prescriptions and daily appointments.

Medical Records

Store patient reports, diagnoses and treatment history securely in one centralized system.

Pharmacy Management

Digital prescriptions, medicine inventory and pharmacy operations from a single dashboard.

Patient Portal

Patients can book appointments, download reports and monitor treatment progress.

Secure Authentication

JWT authentication, encrypted passwords and role-based access for every healthcare user.
