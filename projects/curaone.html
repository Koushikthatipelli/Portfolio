PROJECT OVERVIEW

One Healthcare Platform.
Endless Possibilities.

CuraOne was designed to simplify hospital operations by bringing patients, doctors, pharmacies and administrators together in one modern digital ecosystem focused on accessibility, security and efficiency.

MY ROLE

Full Stack Developer

Designed the UI/UX, developed frontend components, backend APIs, authentication and database architecture.

PROJECT TYPE

Hospital Management

Complete healthcare platform supporting multiple user roles, appointment booking and digital medical management.

TARGET USERS

Patients & Doctors

Built for hospitals, clinics, healthcare professionals and patients seeking a seamless digital healthcare experience.

CORE FEATURES

Smart Healthcare.
Connected Care.

CuraOne combines healthcare management, appointment scheduling, pharmacy services and secure medical records into one intelligent platform.

Appointment Booking

Schedule doctor appointments, manage availability and receive instant booking confirmations.

Doctor Dashboard

Manage patients, appointments, prescriptions and consultation history from one dashboard.

Medical Records

Securely store patient reports, diagnoses, prescriptions and treatment history.

Pharmacy Module

Manage medicine inventory, digital prescriptions and order tracking efficiently.

Patient Portal

Access appointments, prescriptions, reports and personal health records anytime.

Secure Authentication

JWT authentication with role-based access control for every healthcare user.

DEVELOPMENT CHALLENGES

Engineering A Reliable
Healthcare Platform.

Building CuraOne required solving real-world healthcare challenges including security, authentication, appointment scheduling and efficient medical data management.

Secure Authentication

Implemented JWT authentication, encrypted passwords and role-based access for patients, doctors and administrators.

Appointment Scheduling

Developed an appointment management system allowing patients to book, cancel and reschedule consultations.

Medical Record Security

Structured patient records securely while ensuring quick accessibility for authorized healthcare staff.

Backend Architecture

Built scalable REST APIs, optimized MongoDB collections and designed reusable backend services.
