PROJECT OVERVIEW

One Platform.
Complete Developer Journey.

Roadmap.io provides a structured learning experience for aspiring developers by combining technology roadmaps, project-based learning, notes, progress tracking and career preparation into one modern platform.

My Role

Designed and developed the complete platform including UI/UX, frontend architecture, backend APIs, database integration and deployment.

Duration

Planned, designed and developed over six weeks following an agile workflow with continuous improvements and testing.

Platform

Responsive learning platform featuring roadmaps, notes, task management, project tracking and developer resources.

Security

Secure JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, protected REST APIs and MongoDB cloud database.

THE PROBLEM

Learning Full Stack Is Often Confusing.

Most students rely on scattered YouTube videos, blogs and online courses without a proper learning sequence. This leads to confusion, missed concepts and inconsistent progress.

  • No structured learning path
  • Too many disconnected resources
  • Difficult progress tracking
  • Lack of project guidance
THE SOLUTION

Roadmap.io Guides Every Learning Step.

A complete developer platform where users follow structured roadmaps, build practical projects, manage notes and track their progress from beginner to professional Full Stack Developer.

  • Interactive Technology Roadmaps
  • Integrated Notes Workspace
  • Daily Task & Progress Tracking
  • Real-World Project Learning
